About Us

Welcome to
Atitlán Sunset Lodge

Located in Santa Cruz La Laguna, 10 minutes from Panajachel, accessible only by boat. Rent seven independent apartments with beautiful views of Lake Atitlan. Each apartment has Starlink WIFI, a fully equipped kitchen, a spacious and comfortable living area, a private bathroom and terrace overlooking the lake. Enjoy your privacy in a quiet bay.

IMPORTANT: Access is by boat only, the place is very safe but we recommend you to arrive before nightfall as there is no street lighting and it is not easy to find your way. You can’t drive to our place. From November to December the sunset is around 18:00 hrs, from January to June around 18:30 hrs and in June at 19:30 hrs. It is important to note that the sun sets in 15 minutes. If your arrival is after sunset, the only way to get here is by private boat and this must be reserved in advance. If your arrival is before sunset, you can take a public boat to Santa Cruz. You must tell it to bring you to Atitán Sunset Lodge.
